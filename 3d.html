Scan with Laser probe arm

The objective of this project is to scan the current sanitary-ware, analyze the deformation, and provide the corrected 3d model. After carefully scan and rebuild the product, I analyzed the defects and summarized the deformation into an engineering guidance, along with the improved 3D model ready to be made into mould for automatic production.
