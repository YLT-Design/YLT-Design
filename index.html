About me

Grew up in Taiwan, I started my career as a deisgner with focus of consumer electronics and small home appliances after graduated from college with degree in Industrial Design. After being admitted to RIT, along with training of Scandinavian furniture design in Denmark, I have developed a great capability to create designs that embrace humility, user experience, and value.

On my current position, I also take lead in color & finish development, while assist in strategetic planning. This combination equips me with best ability to provide service to sustain our success in the market and keep exceeding customers' expectations.
